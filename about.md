I received my first Tarot deck as a gift in 2008. It was a deck based on the secret drawings used by the **Hermetic Order of the Golden Dawn**.

Today, I am mostly interested in the **Tarot de Marseille**. I treat it as a spiritual path, a tool for divination, and a work of art. 

For me, the structure of the Tarot is like a cathedral: its symbols rise from the Earth (the first cards) all the way to the sky (the Star, Moon, Sun, Judgement, and World cards).
